Causes of ACEs : Discrimination

Discrimination is when a person is treated unfairly because of who they are.

Few basis of discrimination

Disability

Racism

Religion or Belief

Sex / Gender

Discrimination can be direct or indirect

Diret Discrimination

Direct discrimination is when a child is treated differently (at school or in their community) because of one or more of the above characteristics.

indirect Discrimination

Indirect discrimination is when a child is treated in the same way as others, but it has an adverse effect on that child because of who they are. So, for example if a school policy is applied in the same way to everyone but, as a result, puts a disabled pupil at a disadvantage.

Being discriminated against is a risk factor for poor mental health.

Children who experience discrimination may
  • Feel different to other children or 'less than'
  • have an impact on their feeling of belongingness
  • have low self esteem or self worth
  • feel powerless and frustrated
  • have reduced aspirations
  • struggle to reach their full potentials,
