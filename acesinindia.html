ACE in India - Prevelance

India accounts for nearly 15% of the global mental, neurological and substance abuse disorder burden.

ACEs in India

Available data suggests that the point prevalence of depression/affective disorders ranges from 1.2% to 21% in the clinic based studies, 3% - 68% in school based studies and 0.1 % to 6.94% in community studies.


According to statistics reported by ABC News, nearly 30% of students are either bullies or victims of bullying.


About 1,35,000 people die by suicide in India every year. According to WHO data, the suicide rate in India is 16.4 per 1,00,000 for women and 25.8 per 1,00,000 for men.

All such situations have an adverse effect on the emotional well being of the children in the long run. As a result, various behaviours of the children in the future are an effect of the ACE they had.
