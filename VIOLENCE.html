Causes of ACEs : Violence

Children who live in societies witnessing violence may have high levels of behavioural and emotional problems.

The mental health of children is negatively affected by exposure to mass murders and acts of terror like kidnappings, bombings and decapitations.

e.g. : The mental health of the children residing in conflicting areas of Kashmir can be adversely affected.
